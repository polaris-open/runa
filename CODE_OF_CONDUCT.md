# Code of Conduct

Runa follows the Polaris organization-wide Code of Conduct. The canonical,
up-to-date version lives in
[`polaris-open/.github`](https://github.com/polaris-open/.github).

In short:

- Be respectful, patient, and constructive.
- Assume good intent; disagree on ideas, not people.
- No harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Keep discussions focused and welcoming to newcomers.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it through the
channels described in the organization-wide policy in `polaris-open/.github`.
Reports are handled confidentially.
